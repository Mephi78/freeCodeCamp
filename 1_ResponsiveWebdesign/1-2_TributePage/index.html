Lora Ipsumatra

As part of our series about outstanding women of the past still impacting generations of entrepreneurs around the world meet Lora Ipsumatra, born in the late 20s to Vera and Magnus Ipsumatra as their third and youngest daughter and founder of the everlasting Skate & Bant event.

Lora Ipsumatra had always been a vivacious and quirky nature enthusiast with a heart as big as her love for the great outdoors. A true social butterfly, she spent her days chatting with anyone and everyone, often turning mundane conversations into hilarious exchanges that left people in stitches.

Lora's most cherished pastime had been ice skating, which she approached with the same enthusiasm as a child discovering a pile of freshly fallen snow. Whether she was gliding gracefully or hilariously wobbling on the ice, her infectious laughter brought joy to everyone around her.

A true lover of the outdoors, Lora often could be found surrounded by nature, whether she was hiking up a trail or admiring flowers in bloom. However, her love for the great outdoors sometimes clashed hilariously with her social nature, as she tried to balance her love for deep conversations with her desire to avoid pesky mosquito bites.

In her quest to merge her two passions—nature and ice skating— in 1933 Lora founded the wildly popular "Skate & Bant" event. Picture this: a magical outdoor ice rink nestled among towering trees, where skaters can twirl and spin while surrounded by the sounds of chirping birds and rustling leaves. The perfect blend of frosty fun and earthy charm, and Lora was the mastermind behind it all, proving that you can indeed have your ice and skate it too!

But beneath her cheerful demeanor, Lora harbored a secret—she had been head over heels in love with Business Tycoon Amet Dolor, who had captured her heart with his witty humor and knack for turning mundane moments into uproarious adventures.

Long before they finally got happily maried in autumn 1936 and settled down to dedicate most of their time to their beloved family, Amet often took her to business dinners and charity events. In the dry world of investors and entrepreneurs, Lora Ipsumatra had been a breath of fresh air—full of jokes, unexpected falls on the ice, and a heart that beat for both nature and the charming Amet Dolor. Life with Lora had never been dull, and her adventures were sure to leave everyone in stitches!
